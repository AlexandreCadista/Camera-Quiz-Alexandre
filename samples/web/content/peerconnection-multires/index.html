WebRTC samples Peer connection with two video tracks

This demo requests two MediaStreams where the video resolution is different in each track. Both streams are sent on a PeerConnection.
This demo works in Chrome M37 and later.

View the console to see logging. The MediaStream objects localStream1, localStream2, remoteStream1, remoteStream2, and the RTCPeerConnection objects localPeerConnection and remotePeerConnection are in global scope, so you can inspect them in the console as well.

For more information about RTCPeerConnection, see Getting Started With WebRTC.
